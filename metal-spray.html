Metal Wire Flame

Max special tools Company, in cooperation with the founder of Metal Wire Flame in Iran (Mr. Morteza Bajlan) and with a lot of experience, prepares itself in the field of rebuilding all parts of industrial machines, such as mechanical seals, shafts, housings, flanges and all parts used in industries. It announces cement, power plant, steel, etc.

The restoration of parts is done using advanced metal wire flame technology (Surface Coating Technology). In this process, the desired metal or alloy is selected according to the required technical specifications based on the type of part and is melted by gas and sprayed on the surface of the part in the form of fine particles with high speed and impact. During this process, the temperature of the work piece does not exceed 180Â°C, so there is no stress on the piece and the piece is not subjected to physical changes.

According to the variety of raw materials in the form of wire, metal wire flame is capable of compensating wear and tear and creating about 130 types of coatings in the following classification:

  • Stainless steels
  • Non-ferrous metals including copper and aluminum and...
  • Carbon steels

The applications of the above-mentioned coatings are very wide, and in short, the coatings resistant to wear, impact, heat, oxidation, acidic and chemical substances, and the coatings used in surface hardening can be mentioned.

External surfaces of rollers, crankshafts, turbine and compressor shafts, hydraulic and electromechanical axes, internal surfaces of cylinders, shells, bearings, etc. are examples that are repaired by this method.
